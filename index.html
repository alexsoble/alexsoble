Language Learning + YouTube

Language learning is the most fun when it's set to music. That's the idea behind Heyu, a web app I'm building with language teachers and students. Heyu lets anyone turn any YouTube video into a language study resource. Check it out at heyuvideo.com.

Technologies:  Ruby on Rails, Coffeescript.

Divvy Braggin'

I'm a huge bikeshare fan. Shortly after Chicago launched its Divvy bikeshare system, I created an app to let members track their milage. The app was blocked by Divvy's parent company but started a conversation about creating a legitimate API for Chicago bikeshare data. Read the DNAinfoChicago story about DivvyBrags.

Technologies:  Ruby on Rails, Nokogiri web scraping.

Chicago's Local School Councils

Two tweets from the community sparked a new web app for the Chicago Public Schools system, which went on to win "Best Use of Twitter" from Crain's Chicago Business. Read the piece in Crain's and the Storify.

Technologies:  Listening carefully, Google Fusion Tables.
